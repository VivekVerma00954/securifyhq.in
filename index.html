Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

❓ How does SecurifyHQ actually work?

We connect to your existing cameras via secure VPN. Our AI filters false alarms (animals, shadows, etc.), then trained operators review genuine alerts within minutes. If it's a real threat, we contact your emergency contacts and coordinate with authorities if needed. Everything is documented professionally.

📹 What cameras do you support?

We work with most IP cameras that support RTSP streaming: CP Plus, Hikvision, Dahua, TP-Link Tapo, and most generic cameras. We can also work with cloud-enabled cameras. During setup, we'll verify compatibility with your specific cameras.

⚙️ Do I need special equipment?

For most customers, we provide a small pre-configured router (included in setup fee) that creates a secure connection to our monitoring center. You just plug it into your existing internet router. Setup takes about 5 minutes. If you already have compatible cloud cameras, we may not need any hardware at all.

⏱️ How quickly do you respond to alerts?

Basic: Verification begins within 15 minutes
Standard: Verification begins within 5-8 minutes
Premium: Verification begins within 3-5 minutes (priority queue)
Once verified as genuine, escalation to your contacts happens immediately.

🌍 I live in New Zealand/Australia/UK. Can you monitor my India property?

Absolutely! That's exactly what we're designed for. SecurifyHQ serves NRIs worldwide — whether you're in New Zealand, Australia, Canada, UK, USA, or anywhere else. Our NZ-based company with India operations bridges the distance for you.

🔒 Is my camera footage secure?

Yes. We use encrypted VPN connections. Your footage is only accessed when reviewing alerts. We retain recordings for 7-30 days (depending on plan) for incident documentation, then automatically delete them. We never share your footage with third parties.

💰 Are there any hidden fees?

No. You pay: (1) Monthly subscription based on your plan, (2) One-time setup fee (NZ$100-200), and optionally (3) Add-ons you choose. That's it. All prices shown include everything. No activation fees, no surprise charges. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

👮 Do you call the police for me?

On Standard and Premium plans, yes — if you authorize us to do so. When we verify a genuine threat (break-in, intrusion, etc.), we can contact local police on your behalf and provide them with evidence. This requires your explicit authorization during onboarding.

📞 What if I get too many false alerts?

Our AI filters out 80% of false alarms (animals, shadows, weather, etc.) before they even reach our operators. Each plan includes verified alert limits (30/75/150 per month). If you exceed this, you can either optimize camera sensitivity or upgrade to the next tier. We help you tune settings during setup.

🚀 How quickly can I get started?

Most customers are fully operational within 3-5 days: Day 1: Sign up and provide camera info. Days 2-3: We ship pre-configured hardware (if needed). Day 4: You plug it in, we verify connection. Day 5: Monitoring begins! For cloud cameras, it can be even faster (1-2 days).

❌ Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. If you cancel, you keep the hardware (it's yours). Annual plans receive 10% discount but are non-refundable (you get full service for the year you paid for).
