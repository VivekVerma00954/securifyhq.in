1. Introduction & Company Details

This Privacy Policy explains how SECURIFYHQ LIMITED ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our security monitoring services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you sign up for our services, we collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number(s)
  • Property address in India
  • Emergency contact details
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

2.2 Video Surveillance Data

As part of our monitoring service, we collect and process:

  • Video footage from your security cameras
  • Motion detection alerts and event logs
  • Timestamps and incident records
  • Camera metadata (location, settings)

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP addresses and browser information
  • Device information and access logs
  • VPN connection data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Monitor your cameras 24/7 and verify alerts
  • Contact you and emergency contacts when incidents occur
  • Generate incident reports and documentation
  • Set up and maintain your monitoring configuration

3.2 Communication

  • Send service notifications and incident alerts
  • Provide customer support
  • Send monthly review reports
  • Notify you of service updates

3.3 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with New Zealand and Indian data protection laws
  • Respond to law enforcement requests (with proper authorization)
  • Maintain records as required by law

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract Performance: To provide the monitoring service you have subscribed to
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate our business and ensure security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly consented (e.g., marketing)

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • WireGuard VPN for secure camera connections
  • Multi-factor authentication for operator access
  • Regular security audits and access controls

5.2 Data Retention

  • Video Footage: 7–30 days depending on plan, then automatically deleted
  • Incident Reports: Retained for 12 months
  • Account Information: Retained while active, plus 12 months after cancellation
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years (NZ legal requirement)

6. Who We Share Data With

6.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe – payment processing
  • Cloud Hosting Provider – server infrastructure
  • Email Service – customer communications

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.

6.2 Emergency Contacts

We share incident information with your designated emergency contacts when security events occur.

6.3 Law Enforcement

We may disclose information to law enforcement when required by valid legal process or to prevent harm. We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

7. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and GDPR (where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction – request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion – request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability – request transfer to another provider
  • Objection – object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent – for marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at nri@securifyhq.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Complaints

If your concern is not resolved, you may contact the NZ Privacy Commissioner at www.privacy.org.nz or 0800 803 909.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred between New Zealand (company registration) and India (monitoring operations and camera locations). We ensure appropriate safeguards through encryption during transit, secure access controls, and contractual protections.

9. Video Surveillance Specific Provisions

9.1 Your Responsibilities

  • You must have legal authority to install cameras at the monitored property
  • You must comply with local privacy laws regarding video surveillance in India
  • You must inform visitors/occupants that surveillance is in place
  • Cameras must not infringe on neighbors' privacy or record public areas

9.2 Our Monitoring

  • Operators view footage only when alerts are triggered or during scheduled reviews
  • Footage is not viewed continuously (event-based review only)
  • All viewed footage is handled with strict confidentiality

9.3 Footage Requests

You can request copies of your video footage at any time. We will provide footage within 48 hours of your request.

10. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only for session management and basic functionality. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be emailed to active customers with 30 days notice. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
